Bindings for Ingenic JZ47xx family of SoCs

Required properties for root node:
- compatible: One of:
  * ingenic,jz4740
  * ingenic,jz4725b
  * ingenic,jz4770
  * ingenic,jz4780

Required properties for CPU nodes:
- compatible: One of:
  * ingenic,xburst-d0
  * ingenic,xburst-d1
  * ingenic,xburst-e1
